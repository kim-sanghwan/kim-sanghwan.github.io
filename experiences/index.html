My Experiment Page

Research Assistant at Advanced Interactive Technologies Lab, ETH Zürich

July 2023 - Present

I am working on Long-Term Action Anticipation (LTA) task utilizing vision-language and large language models, under the supervision of Dr. Xi Wang and Dr. Yongqin Xian. We achieved the state-of-the-art performance on LTA Ego4D challenge.

Research Assistant at KrauthammerLab, University of Zürich

June 2022 - March 2023

I improved the transformer-based models on radiology report generation task from chest X-ray images, which was accepted at ACL 2023, BioNLP Workshop. I worked closely with Dr. Farhad Nooralahzadeh and PI. Michael Krauthammer. We also collaborated with radiologists at Kyoto University to understand radiology reports.

Research Assistant at Data Analytics Lab, ETH Zürich

March 2021 - May 2021

I developed a deep learning emulator of complex cosmological simulations to constrain the source of gravitational waves, under the supervision of Prof. Aurelien Lucchi and Prof. Thomas Hofmann. We also collaborated with Prof. Anastasios Fragkos, University of Geneva to interpret the results in astrophysical respect.

Machine Learning Intern at Samsung Electronics

Jan 2020 - Feb 2020

I designed new loss function for multitask learning on semantic segmentation and depth regression, which was organized as a full paper.
